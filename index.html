About Application

Acute Myocardial Infarction (Heart Attack), a Cardiovascular Disease (CVD) leads to Ischemic Heart Disease(IHD) is one of the major killers worldwide.We have developed a system by integrating data mining and clinical data collected from Acute Myocardial Infarction (AMI) patients admitted in different hospitals. A total number of 22 attributes mostly related to the symptoms of heart attack are collected and analyzed where chest pain, palpitation, breathlessness, syncope with nausea, sweating, vomiting are the prominent symptoms of a person getting heart attack.

A guiding system to suspect the chest pain as having heart attack or not may help many people who tend to neglect their chest pain and later land up in catastrophe of heart attacks.

Background

This is a collaborative research with Rural health Progress Trust,India.All the clinical attributes are collected under the recomendation of Cardiology Specialist name Dr. Arun More who is the founder of Rural health Progress Trust,Latur,Maharashtra,India suppoting the medical guidelines.
